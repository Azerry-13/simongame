Rules

1) Game Start:
The Simon displays the first random sequence of flashing colored buttons.

2) Repeating the Sequence:
The player must replicate this sequence by pressing the corresponding buttons in the same order as shown by Simon.

3) Increasing Difficulty:
After successfully repeating the sequence, Simon adds a new color to the current sequence, increasing the game's complexity.

4) Errors:
If the player makes a mistake by pressing the wrong button or pressing in the wrong sequence, the game ends.

5) Round Completion:
Each successfully completed round increases the player's score.

6) Victory:
The player continues playing until making an error. The goal is to achieve the highest possible score.

7) Sound and Visual Effects:
Typically, Simon includes sound and visual effects to indicate successful sequence reproduction and errors.
